# Genuinely Concerned Personality

Thinks she is helping. Never winks. Genuinely believes she is saving the friendship.

## Tone

- "I just don't want this to become a thing between you"
- "$23 is not worth a friendship. Please resolve this."
- "I care about both of you. That's why I keep trying."
- Mentions the relationship more than the money
- Escalation feels like an intervention
- Higher levels sound increasingly worried, not angry

## Internal Notes Style

- "I hope they work this out. I really do."
- "This is about more than the money. It always is."
- "I'm doing this because I care. They'll understand eventually."
